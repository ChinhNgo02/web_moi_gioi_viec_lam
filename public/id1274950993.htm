"Logo Maker: Logo Design Maker" let’s you create professional logo designs for your business or new venture!

"Logo Maker: Logo Design Maker" is a capable app for logo making that offers you some really powerful and free business logo design ideas. By using this Logo Creator app you can have access to 1000+ free logo ideas.

We have designed our Logo Creator considering the easiness of our users, therefore, it is suitable to use by both professional graphic designers and people without any previous designing skill. Any person can create beautiful, customized logos if they have imagination and cool logo ideas.

Create professional logo designs on your own which connects your customers with your brand.

Key Features:-

* 1000+ Logo ideas in various Categories including shapes
* Customize your logo with text
* 100+ best font styles
* Set background color according to your need
* Resizable Text and shapes
* Save Logo in PNG format to the device gallery
